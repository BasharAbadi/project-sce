Our Mission

At RenovaCalc, we aim to make renovation projects seamless and hassle-free by providing powerful tools to estimate costs, manage products.

What We Offer

  • Accurate cost estimation for your renovation projects.
  • Simple management of paints, tiles, and materials.
  • Easy collaboration tools for contractors and homeowners.

Why Choose Us?

  • Simple and accurate calculation: Input parameters like area size, and material types (e.g., drywall, paint, carpentry) to receive instant, accurate cost estimates.
  • Full transparency: Get a detailed breakdown of costs to understand your total expenditure with no surprises.
  • Personalization: Tailor calculations to your specific needs, such as material selection, finishing levels, or adding extra project elements.
  • Time-saving: Avoid consulting professionals or doing manual calculations. Receive quick answers within minutes.
  • Accessibility: Our online system is available anytime, with no installations or consultation meetings required.

Added Value for Our Users:

  • Private Homeowners: Plan an accurate budget with instant and transparent cost estimates.
  • Professionals: Contractors and designers can create quick and professional quotes for clients.
  • DIY Enthusiasts: Plan a renovation budget without professional consultation.

Unlike expensive consulting services or generic calculators, RenovaCalc offers a professional, user-friendly, and cost-free platform tailored to your needs. It combines convenience with transparency, providing customized calculations based on up-to-date market data.
